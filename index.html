Our Science Units

Each unit below connects to your interactive notebook and includes the big questions we're investigating.

🧪 Physical Science

Matter & Its Interactions

Investigating properties of matter, mixtures, and chemical vs. physical change.

🌱 Life Science

Ecosystems & Energy Flow

Exploring how matter and energy move through organisms, food webs, and ecosystems.

🌎 Earth Science

Earth's Systems

Studying the geosphere, hydrosphere, atmosphere, and biosphere and how they interact.

🌌 Space Systems

Stars & the Solar System

Modeling patterns of the sun, moon, stars, and planets in our sky and solar system.

🌦️ Earth Science

Weather & Climate

Analyzing weather data and patterns to understand climate over time.

⚙️ Engineering

Engineering Design

Defining problems and designing, testing, and improving solutions like real engineers.

Vocabulary Lab

Search or scroll to review key terms from our units.

EcosystemA community of living things interacting with their physical environment.
HypothesisA testable explanation for an observation or question.
MatterAnything that has mass and takes up space.
EvidenceData or observations used to support a claim.
VariableA factor in an experiment that can change or be changed.
ErosionThe process of rock and soil being worn away by wind, water, or ice.
PhotosynthesisThe process plants use to turn sunlight into energy.
OrbitThe curved path an object takes around another object in space.
